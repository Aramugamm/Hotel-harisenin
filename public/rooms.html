Discover Our Rooms!

Experience comfort and luxury with our range of meticulously designed rooms. Whether you're looking for a cozy retreat or upscale elegance, we have the perfect accommodation to suit your needs. From stylish standard rooms to opulent suites, each space is crafted to provide a serene haven during your stay. Enjoy modern amenities such as high-speed Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and indulgent bath facilities. Our dedicated team is committed to ensuring your satisfaction and making your stay truly memorable. Explore our room options below and elevate your stay with us.

Explore

Two King Bedrooms

Gratify yourself with the privacy and ample space of our Two King Bed guest rooms, which feature two distinct bedrooms, each furnished with a luxurious king-size bed. You can also relish a complimentary cup of coffee while watching your favorite shows on one of our premium LED TVs. This suite ensures that everyone enjoys the room they require in a truly lavish manner.

One King Bed Executive Suite

Designed with luxury in mind, this remarkably roomy and contemporary suite offers a delightful experience! The King Executive Suite features a king-sized bed along with a distinct living space and a dining/conference area, ensuring the utmost in comfort. The sofa bed allows for a tranquil stay accommodating up to four individuals. A Complimentary American Breakfast Buffet is also included.

Queen Standard Room

This room offers simplicity and comfort, featuring a Queen-sized bed and suitable for up to two guests. It’s an ideal choice for business travelers, thanks to its stylish desk, and it’s also convenient for families, as it comes equipped with a mini fridge, microwave, and coffee maker, allowing you to store and reheat leftovers from local restaurants. This standard room is a fantastic option for those seeking affordable travel without compromising essential amenities.

Queen Standard ADA Room

This room offers a simple and inviting atmosphere, featuring a Queen-sized bed and suitable for two guests. It’s well-suited for business travelers due to its chic work desk, and it’s also convenient for families, as it provides a mini fridge, microwave, and coffee maker, allowing you to save and reheat restaurant leftovers. This standard room presents an excellent choice for those seeking budget-friendly travel without compromising essential amenities.

Two Double Bed Suite

Perfect for accommodating the whole family, this suite provides two double beds and a sofa bed, making it possible for up to six guests to experience the best of the New York / New Jersey area. Combining generous living areas with affordability, this roomy and convenient suite guarantees ample space for everyone to relax and unwind.

Two Double Bed Suite (ADA)

The Two Double Bed ADA Suite boasts a separate living space complete with a sofa bed, providing comfort for up to 4 occupants. This suite features an open living arrangement and includes amenities like a refrigerator, microwave, and coffee maker. You’ll find HDTVs in each room, and the bathroom is equipped with a marble vanity and premium amenities for your enjoyment.

One King Bed Suite

The King Bed Suite combines both practicality and distinct areas, featuring a roomy bedroom with a king-size bed and a separate living space furnished with a sofa bed. This suite is suitable for accommodating up to four individuals and comes with premium amenities to enhance the experience for the whole family.

One King Bed Suite (ADA)

The King Bed Suite (ADA) provides a blend of practicality, separation, and accessibility, featuring a generously sized bedroom with a king-size bed and a separate living area complete with a sofa bed. This suite is designed to comfortably host up to four guests and is equipped with premium amenities to enhance the experience for the whole family.
